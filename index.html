A little about me

Hey, my name is Grace and I'm a Computer Engineering student at the University of Waterloo. My current focus is full stack development with an emphasis on front-end.

For Winter 2017, I worked as a full stack developer for Imagine Communications in Waterloo, ON. I was primarily working on Onyx, a cloud-based playlist automation solution for TV stations such as ABC and Disney. I worked extensively with AngularJS, TypeScript and C#.

For Spring 2016, I worked at RBC developing an ASP.NET application to manage their databases. I've added support for Microsoft SQL Server, Sybase, Oracle and DB2.

Aside from programming, I've always been passionate about art, and I work as a freelance artist; have a look at some of my artwork below! I also enjoy playing piano and Smash (4).
